**Mark Fisher** (1968 – 2017) was a co-founder of Zer0 Books and, later, Repeater Books. His blog, k-punk, defined critical writing for a generation. He wrote three books, _Capitalist Realism_, _Ghosts of My Life_ and _The Weird and the Eerie_, and was a Visiting Fellow in the Visual Cultures department at Goldsmiths, University of London.

**Darren Ambrose** is a freelance writer and editor from the North-East of England.

**Simon Reynolds** is the author of _Retromania_ and _Rip It Up and Start Again_.
